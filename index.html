About this page

Guideline in the selection of these phrases: A phrase should not say it all and leave some space for the reader. It needs to be somewhat timeless. It should be an idea that spreads, yet not have become too much of a cliché. Selection is everything. When in doubt, remove.

There is no particular order in these quotes. Sometimes, a commentary is added, indicated by '---'. This can be a tangential thought, an association, a bit of context, an interpretation, ...
